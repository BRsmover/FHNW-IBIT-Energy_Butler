The Basic Business Idea

A few Highlights

Our software – Energy Butler - connects all your smart devices inside your home and allows you to control your devices.

  • Create device groups
  • Create consumption reports
  • Benchmarking per device type
  • Turning smart devices on or off via smartphone

And the best: energy saving is done on the fly!

We offer you a cloud-based service which can be accessed on your mobile device, available for all Apple and Android smart phone (Jelly Bean or higher). In addition you may connect yourself with the devices via our secured homepage.

You will be able to check the statistics about the usage and energy consumption of your smart home as well as a benchmark for your devices. With this newly acquired information you will be able to compare the energy consumption of your devices with our benchmarks.

Define a time frame in which devices should be automatically turn on / off by your Energy Butler. You don’t need to turn them off manually!
